PWP Milestone 2

Content Strategy

My site will be one page, and will consist of the following sections:

Navbar
A logo I made for myself and links to the sections below.
Welcome
A brief introduction that clearly states my name and role as a Fullstack + Java Developer.
Recent Experience
This section will detail my professional history, core skill set, and professional highlights. This section will showcase my strengths and personality through my achievements at Deep Dive and Verizon. I will use a contrasting "TL;DR" sidebar to list my core skills and programming languages which will be easily readable at a glance.
Front-end Portfolio
I want this to be an image carousel containing 4-6 examples of my best work. Tapping each image will expand it to show details including my specific contributions and the technology used.
Back-end Portfolio
I need to research what a Java portfolio should look like. My current concept is a grid of Bootstrap cards with a thumbnail and/or brief description, which expand on click to show relevant details.
Contact Me
This section will contain a simple contact form, my professional email address, and additional links to my professional profiles on LinkedIn and GitHub. (These will also be linked to in the footer site wide via icons.)
