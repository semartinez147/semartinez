PWP Milestone 1

Purpose

My PWP will be a portfolio site to supplement my resume and attract potential clients/employers. It will replace the current GitHub Sites version of my portfolio, and will probably incorporate my Java projects as well.

As a stretch goal or post-bootcamp project, it may also link to a functioning interface for my personal project from Deep Dive Java.

Audience

The target audience for my website is recruiters, hiring managers, and clients.

Goals

  • To showcase my programming work and self-advertise.
  • To acquire full-time or freelance employment.

Persona

Grace Phillips

Small Business Owner

Details

  • Runs a boutique selling locally-sourced products.
  • Has a Facebook page for her business.
  • Wants to grow by acquiring new partners and customers.
  • Does not like working with technology.

Frustrations

  • Lack of customizability in Facebook business page.
  • Reliance on Facebook Messenger.
  • Facebook ads on/near her page she can't control.

Needs

  • A site accessible from any device.
  • Simple interface to learn about or purchase goods.
  • Tools to track sales and individual customers.
  • Less time spent managing the technology side of her business.
